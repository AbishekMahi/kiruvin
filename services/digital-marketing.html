Google AdWords

Google AdWords is the platform where advertisers bid on keywords that people search for using Google's search engine. An advertiser buys a keyword to appear when someone searches for that specific word. If a person clicks on the ad, they are taken to the website and if the visitor makes a purchase, then the advertiser gets paid.

Facebook Ads Management

The Facebook Ads Manager is where you manage your ad campaigns. It's a tool that helps you manage all aspects of your campaign and provide analytics on how well each campaign is performing. The first step towards any successful advertising campaign is understanding what type of audience you have. You need to know who your target market is and how they use social media. Once you understand your audience, it's time to create ads that appeal to them. Depending on your budget and objective, you may run paid campaigns or free campaigns.

Search Engine Optimization

Search engine optimization (SEO) is the practice of increasing the visibility of a website or webpage in search engines via the “natural” or un-paid ("organic") search results. SEO techniques vary across search engines; however, they may involve the use of keywords, links, meta tags, site structure and internal linking pages. In general, the search engines place greater value upon the relevance of sites containing useful content than commercial intent. SEO aims to provide the best user experience possible given the users search terms.

Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing is the process of using online platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn to build relationships and interact with potential and current customers.
While traditional marketing focuses on broadcasting a message to a large audience, social media marketing is all about engaging with customers and fostering two-way communication.
The goal of social media marketing is to build relationships and trust with customers, which will ultimately lead to increased sales and brand loyalty.

Social Media Management

Social media management is the process of monitoring and managing social networks such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Snapchat, YouTube, etc. These platforms allow users to share news, pictures, videos, etc. from their phone, computer, tablet, or other devices. Most people use these platforms everyday to stay connected with friends, family members, and businesses.
As a cannabis business owner, you have to make sure that you're able to keep your audience engaged. If they don't feel interested in what you post, they won't want to follow you on any of your social networks. You need to create posts that engage them and get them excited about what you do.

Email marketing

Email marketing is a great way to contact your customers and keep them engaged with your brand. You can send emails to your subscribers informing them about upcoming events, promotions, sales, and any other updates. Emails can help improve your customer retention rate and increase your brand awareness. A well-crafted email campaign can attract new customers while reminding existing ones about your business.

Content Marketing

Content marketing is a form of marketing focused on creating, publishing, and distributing content for a targeted audience online. It is often used in conjunction with other forms of digital marketing, such as SMM, email marketing, and SEO.
Content marketing is an effective way to build brand awareness, generate leads, and drive sales. When done correctly, it can also help you create meaningful relationships with your customers and create a loyal fan base.

Website Promotion

Website Promotion is the process of driving traffic to your website through organic or paid means. The primary goal of website promotion is to increase visibility and awareness of your website, so that more people can visit and interact with it.
There are many different ways to promote your website, and the most effective approach will depend on your budget, your target audience, and your goals. However, some common website promotion strategies include search engine optimization (SEO), search engine marketing (SEM), social media marketing, and email marketing.

Link Building

Link building refers to any activities undertaken by a website owner to generate external links pointing back to their website. Common link building tactics include submitting articles to article directory sites, guest posting on Eventss, and engaging in social networking. Many times, link building involves offering a reciprocal link in exchange for a hyperlink.
